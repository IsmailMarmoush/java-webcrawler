English Pronouns List

A pronoun is a word that replaces one or more nouns.

Note that some pronouns from the list below can be used as other parts of speech. What part of speech it is depends on how the word is used within a sentence. Look at Pronoun or Adjective quiz to understand how Demonstrative, Interrogative, and Indefinite pronouns can act as adjectives. In addition, look at the Personal Pronouns Quiz and Possessive adjectives section of the pronouns page to understand the difference between possessive pronouns and possessive adjectives (determiners).
Try Spelling Quiz on English Pronouns.
For more quizzes on Pronouns go to What is a Pronoun.
